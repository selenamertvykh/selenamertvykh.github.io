You should probably become a weapon

2022-05-29
It’s morning again in America. We live in the future. O tempora! O mores! Brynn Tannehill was right, although her timeline may have been a little pessimistic. If you’re like me at all, a sizable fraction of the population wants you dead, as in lined up and shot. They will unblinkingly accuse your suicide hotlines of being sexual predators. If you’re unlucky enough to draw the attention of some of them, they will harass you until you commit die — dox you, show up outside your house, get you fired from your job — for the crime of being trans on the internet, because they think it’s funny.
